SEAMUN I Conference

SEAMUN I is the inaugural SEAMUN conference hosted at the AIT Conference Center in Pathum Thani, Thailand. Join 200 delegates, 22 chairs, 8 SMT, 10 staff, and 10 external advisors for two days of policy-driven debate.

Why SEAMUN I

Policies with a Purpose brings together delegates from grades 7-12 to debate human rights, security, health, and fantasy scenarios with guided support and accessible facilities.

Inclusive participation

Independent delegates are welcome. Advisors are optional.

Accessible venue

Wheelchair accessible and sensory-friendly accommodations.

10 committees

Traditional, specialized, and a Fantasy World Committee.

Unique topics

From Arctic ocean acidification to peacekeeping best practices.

Committees

Ten committees with curated topics and skill levels. Chairs are TBD for all committees.
