How to use this page

Grade — Filter by school year (7–8, 9–10, 11–12). Pick your grade to see committees you can join.

Type — Traditional (UN bodies), Specialized (e.g. WHO, Press), or Fantasy (fictional universe).

Each card shows topics, number of chairs and delegates, and Show allocations lists which countries/roles you can be assigned.

Committee

A group that debates one or two topics (e.g. UNHRC, WHO). Each has chairs and a set number of delegate spots (allocations).

Grade filter

Filter committees by school year. 7–8, 9–10, and 11–12 show which committees are open to each grade level.

Committee type

Traditional — Classic UN bodies (e.g. UNSC, UNHRC, ECOSOC). Specialized — Themed or media committees (WHO, Interpol, Press). Fantasy — Fictional universe (e.g. FWC).

Allocations

The list of countries (or roles, e.g. press agencies) that can be assigned in this committee. You will be allocated one when you register.

Difficulty

Beginner — Good for first-time delegates. Intermediate — Some experience helpful. Advanced — Complex topics and procedure.
