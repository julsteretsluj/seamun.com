Sponsor SEAMUN I

Support a student-led, non-profit Model UN conference. All conference surplus is donated to the King Chulalongkorn Memorial Hospital Foundation.

Ways to support

Monetary sponsorship

One-off or tiered financial support. Funds go toward venue, materials, and the conference donation pledge.

In-kind sponsorship

Venue provision, catering, printing, AV equipment, or other goods and services that reduce conference costs and increase the amount we can donate.

Sponsorship tiers & benefits

Platinum

100,000 THB+

  • Logo on main stage and all materials
  • Mention in opening and closing ceremonies
  • Listing as Platinum Sponsor on website and programme
  • Optional short address or message to delegates

Gold

50,000 – 99,999 THB

  • Logo on website and programme
  • Mention in opening ceremony
  • Listing as Gold Sponsor

Silver

25,000 – 49,999 THB

  • Logo on website and programme
  • Listing as Silver Sponsor

Bronze

10,000 – 24,999 THB

  • Name on website and programme
  • Listing as Bronze Sponsor

In-kind sponsorship is valued at fair market rate. Custom tiers and benefits can be discussed.

Where the money goes

  • Donation pledge — 100% of conference surplus is donated to the King Chulalongkorn Memorial Hospital Foundation.
  • Venue & logistics — Space for 250 participants, 1 main plenary and 10 breakout rooms, AV and basic catering.
  • Delegate experience — Materials, nameplates, and running a smooth two-day conference (23–24 January 2027).
